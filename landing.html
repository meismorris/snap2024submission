Director

Hayao Miyazaki

Hayao Miyazaki, a pioneering Japanese animator and co-founder of Studio Ghibli, is celebrated for his iconic films like "Spirited Away" and "My Neighbor Totoro." His work, known for its imaginative storytelling and profound themes, has earned him widespread acclaim and solidified his legacy as one of the most influential figures in animation history.
